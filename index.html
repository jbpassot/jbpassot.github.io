Our Mission

At Serene, our mission is to transform the lives of millions of teenage girls struggling with mental health issues by providing innovative solutions and rebuilding real-life communities. We are dedicated to addressing the root causes of mental health challenges and empowering young girls to develop supportive relationships. Our approach focuses on three main principles:

  • Targeted Solutions

    We believe in developing tailored solutions that cater specifically to the unique needs of teenage girls. Our programs and tools are designed to resonate with their experiences and challenges, ensuring a more significant impact on their mental health journey.

  • Community Involvement

    Serene recognizes the importance of a strong support system in overcoming mental health struggles. Our approach involves parents, teachers, and the community as primary mentors, working together to create a nurturing and empowering environment for teenage girls.

  • Technology as an Enabler

    We harness the power of technology to facilitate communication, track progress, and provide actionable insights. By utilizing advanced tools, we can effectively monitor mental health trends and offer timely interventions, while still emphasizing the importance of human connection and empathy.

Our commitment to these principles drives us to make a meaningful impact on the lives of teenage girls and their families. Together, we can prevent mental health struggles from escalating and save lives.

Why Serene?

According to the CDC's Youth Risk Behavior Survey, teenage girls are experiencing alarmingly high levels of mental distress, violence, and substance use. Collected in the fall of 2021, the survey highlights that teen girls are facing the highest levels of sexual violence, sadness, and hopelessness ever reported to the YRBS. Nearly 1 in 5 teen girls experienced sexual violence in the past year. Three in five girls felt persistently sad and hopeless in 2021, a substantial increase from 2011. Furthermore, over 1 in 4 girls seriously considered attempting suicide in 2021, a significant increase from 2011, while more than 1 in 10 reported actually attempting suicide that year.

While there are numerous mental health apps available, many of them have limitations in addressing the specific needs of teenage girls. These limitations have inspired Serene to take a different approach, focusing on a comprehensive population health strategy that involves parents, teachers, and the community.

Limitations of the Current Approach in the Mental Health App Industry

Many existing mental health apps lack a targeted approach towards addressing the unique needs and challenges faced by teenage girls. Furthermore, these apps often focus solely on individual users, neglecting the critical role that social support networks, such as family, friends, and educators, can play in mental health and well-being. Additionally, some apps may not be evidence-based or lack proper integration with professional mental health services, which can limit their effectiveness in providing adequate support.

Reasons Behind Serene's Decision to Take a Different Approach

Serene was created with the understanding that teenage girls require a specialized approach to address their mental health needs effectively. This realization, coupled with the limitations of the current mental health app industry, led Serene to develop an innovative approach that emphasizes a supportive ecosystem, evidence-based practices, and professional collaboration. By incorporating these elements, Serene aims to provide a more comprehensive and effective solution for the mental health challenges faced by teenage girls.

The Population Health Approach: Involving Parents, Teachers, and the Community

Serene adopts a population health approach that goes beyond individual users, recognizing the vital role played by parents, teachers, and the community in promoting mental well-being. This approach encourages collaboration and communication between these key stakeholders, fostering a supportive environment for teenage girls to thrive. By involving parents and teachers, Serene helps to create a strong foundation of understanding and empathy, while also equipping them with the tools and resources necessary to support their children and students. Furthermore, by engaging the community, Serene helps to raise awareness and reduce stigma around mental health, creating a more inclusive and supportive atmosphere for teenage girls to seek help and find the support they need.

Our Approach

Serene's approach will revolve around rebuilding real-life communities and fostering strong connections between teenage girls, their parents, teachers, and the community. We believe that by nurturing these relationships, we can create a solid support network for teenage girls, helping them to better navigate the challenges they face.

Overview of Serene's Approach to Rebuilding Real-Life Communities

Serene aims to reestablish and strengthen real-life communities by providing a platform that encourages communication, collaboration, and support.

  • Organizing events and workshops to bring together parents, teachers, and community members, fostering connections and promoting awareness around mental health issues faced by teenage girls.
  • Creating safe spaces for teenage girls to connect with their peers, share their experiences, and support one another through various activities and discussions.
  • Developing evidence-based resources, tools, and educational materials for parents, teachers, and community members to better understand and address the mental health needs of teenage girls.
  • Collaborating with schools, local organizations, and mental health professionals to integrate mental health support into existing structures and systems, ensuring accessible and effective care for teenage girls.

Focus on Parents, Teachers, and the Community as Primary Mentors for Teenage Girls

Parents, teachers, and the community play a crucial role as primary mentors for teenage girls. Serene's approach emphasizes their importance in the following ways:

  • Parents: Serene equips parents with the knowledge, skills, and resources necessary to support their daughters' mental well-being. By providing a platform for parents to connect, share experiences, and learn from one another, Serene fosters a nurturing and understanding environment for teenage girls to thrive.
  • Teachers: Teachers are in a unique position to identify and support students who may be struggling with mental health challenges. Serene works closely with educators, providing them with tools to ensure they are well-equipped to help teenage girls navigate their mental health journey.
  • Community: Serene collaborates with local organizations, mental health professionals, and community leaders to create a supportive and inclusive atmosphere for teenage girls. By raising awareness, reducing stigma, and promoting a holistic approach to mental health, Serene encourages communities to take an active role in supporting the well-being of their teenage girls.

Impact

At Serene, we are committed to making a tangible, positive impact on the mental health and well-being of teenage girls, their families, and the communities in which they live. Through our holistic approach, we will measure our impact in various aspects of the lives of those involved in our programs:

  • Improved Mental Health: Teenage girls participating in Serene programs will report reductions in feelings of sadness, hopelessness, and anxiety. They will also show increased resilience, self-esteem, and overall well-being.
  • Enhanced Relationships: By fostering connections between teenage girls, their parents, teachers, and the community, Serene will contribute to the development of stronger, more supportive relationships, which play a crucial role in promoting mental health.
  • Empowerment and Agency: Our programs and resources will empower teenage girls, parents, teachers, and community members with the knowledge, skills, and confidence to take charge of their mental health and advocate for themselves and others.
  • Increased Awareness and Reduced Stigma: Serene's efforts will raise awareness around mental health issues faced by teenage girls, resulting in reduced stigma and more open conversations about mental health within families, schools, and communities.
  • Positive School and Community Environments: By collaborating with schools and local organizations, Serene will help create safe, supportive, and inclusive environments that prioritize mental health and well-being, contributing to the overall success and happiness of teenage girls.

We continuously measure our impact through data collection, surveys, and feedback from participants, ensuring that our programs and resources remain effective and responsive to the evolving needs of teenage girls and their communities.
